Let the function :math:`f(x) = 6x^2-3x` be as represented above.

The figure is not to scale.

#. Find :math:`\int \! (6x^2-3x) \, \mathrm{d}x`.

#.

   Find the area of the region bounded by the graph of :math:`f(x)`,

   the x-axis, and the lines :math:`x = 1` and :math:`x = 2`.

   That is, :math:`\int_1^2 \! (6x^2-3x) \, \mathrm{d}x`.
