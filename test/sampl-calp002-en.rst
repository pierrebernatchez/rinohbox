A closed cylindrical metal box has a radius of :math:`r` centimetres and a height of :math:`h` centimetres, and a volume of :math:`20\pi\, cm^3`.

The figure is not to scale.

|cylindre|

A) Express :math:`h` in terms of :math:`r`.

The metal for the base and lid of the box costs 10 cents per :math:`cm^2` and the metal for the curved side costs :math:`8` cents per :math:`cm^2`.

The total cost of the metal, in cents, is :math:`C`.

B) Show that :math:`C\,=\,20\pi{}r^2 + \frac{320\pi}{r}`

C) Given that there is a minimum value for :math:`C`, find this minimum value in terms of :math:`\pi`.
