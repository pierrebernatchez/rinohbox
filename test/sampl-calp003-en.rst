Consider a function :math:`f(x)`. The line :math:`L_1` with equation :math:`y = 3x + 1` is tangent to the graph of :math:`f(x)` when :math:`x = 2`.

1.

   #. Write down :math:`f^\prime(2)`.

   #. Find :math:`f(2)`.


Let :math:`g(x) = f(x^2 + 1)` and let :math:`P` be the point on the graph of :math:`g` where :math:`x = 1`.

2.

   Show that the graph of :math:`g` has a slope of 6 at point :math:`P`.


Let :math:`L_2` be the tangent to the graph of :math:`g` at point :math:`P`. :math:`L_1` intersects :math:`L_2` at point :math:`Q`.

3.

   Find the y-coordinate of :math:`Q`.
